    About me

    I am currently a Ph.D. student in the College of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, under the supervision of Qingshan Liu.

    Previously, I earned both my Master's and Bachelor's degrees from Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology, also under the guidance of Qingshan Liu. From July 2021 to February 2023, I worked as an algorithm engineer at Leapmotor Technology

    My current research primarily focuses on general LiDAR perception, including segmentation and detection, and LiDAR scene generation. Here is my personal CV. Feel free to email me if you are interested in.
